About Me

I’m William, a Ph.D. student at the University of Chicago a part of ChiData, where I am advised by Aaron Elmore. I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area and completed my B.A. at the University of California, Berkeley where I was a part of the RISE Lab and worked a variety of amazing projects, such as Ray and Modin!
